Gabriel Aguilar

Gabriel Aguilar is an Immersive Director and Creative Technologist whose work spans live theater, installation, and interactive experience design. Before focusing on immersive work, he spent five years as a professional video editor, cutting branded content for clients including Johnson & Johnson, Mount Sinai, and The Moth Project. This foundation sharpened his instincts for pacing, visual storytelling, and narrative structure.

Drawing on a background that encompasses theatrical production, cinematography, spatial media, and emerging technologies, he creates environments in which the audience's physical presence is the medium. He is currently completing a BTech in Emerging Media Technologies at CUNY City Tech and building a practice centered on immersive performance and projection mapping.
